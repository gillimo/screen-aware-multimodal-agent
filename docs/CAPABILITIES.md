# Coach Capabilities (Exhaustive Draft)

This list defines what the OSRS coach can do. It is intentionally broad and can be trimmed later.

## Account Audit
- Import and validate account state (skills, quests, diaries, combat stats, gear, bank, gp, unlocks).
- Summarize current progression stage (early, mid, late, endgame).
- Detect missing unlocks that block major content.
- Identify highest ROI activities for the next 1-3 sessions.
- Flag risk exposure (death cost, unsafe gear for current content).

## Goal Planning
- Break long goals into milestones (quests, skills, gear, gp).
- Produce daily, weekly, and monthly plans.
- Recommend skill order to unlock major quests.
- Suggest fast-track paths for specific goals (barrows gloves, fire cape, quest cape, diaries).
- Compare multiple goal paths and show tradeoffs.

## Quest Coaching
- Provide quest prerequisite chains.
- Rank quests by unlock impact and time cost.
- Recommend quest order for efficient xp or unlocks.
- Surface item, level, and location requirements.
- Recommend quest bundles for multi-skill progression.

## Achievement Diaries
- Map diary tasks to skill and quest requirements.
- Recommend diary order by unlock value (teleports, profits, QoL).
- Highlight highest impact diary steps for the account.

## Skilling Plans
- Recommend training methods per skill (fast xp, low cost, profit).
- Suggest level targets for unlocks (teleports, items, quests).
- Recommend tool upgrades and skilling outfit goals.
- Identify time-limited or high leverage training routes.

## Combat Readiness
- Evaluate combat stats, gear, and prayer availability.
- Recommend next combat milestones (prayer levels, weapon tiers).
- Suggest defensive and offensive improvement paths.
- Provide safe alternatives for risky fights.

## Slayer Planning
- Recommend masters and block lists by level and gear.
- Suggest task-specific gear and supplies.
- Plan for unlocks (broad bolts, slayer helm, herb sack).

## Bossing Progression
- Rank bosses by readiness and expected profitability.
- Recommend gear prerequisites and mechanics practice steps.
- Provide path from entry bosses to mid/high tier bosses.

## Money Making
- Compare gp per hour options based on stats and gear.
- Recommend low risk vs high risk money routes.
- Track gp targets for gear and quest items.
- Provide alternative methods when gear is missing.

## Gear and Inventory
- Recommend gear upgrades by impact and cost.
- Suggest buy vs craft decisions for key items.
- Identify missing utility items (teleport tabs, stam pots, antidotes).
- Provide gear set templates by activity.

## Teleports and Mobility
- Map teleport network (jewelry, spellbooks, poh, minigames).
- Recommend best teleport unlocks for travel time reduction.
- Identify missing teleport items or spellbook switches.

## Spellbooks and Magic
- Recommend spellbook choices by activity.
- Plan unlocks for ancients, lunars, arceuus.
- Suggest key spells for skilling and combat.

## Prayer and PvM Safety
- Recommend prayer unlocks and training methods.
- Suggest protection prayer usage by boss mechanics.
- Plan for overheads and quick prayers.

## Minigames and Misc Unlocks
- Recommend minigames by unlock value (void, fighter torso, etc).
- Track misc unlocks (ava device, spirit trees, fairy rings).

## Clue Scrolls
- Evaluate clue readiness by skill and quest completion.
- Suggest optimal gear and teleport setups for clue steps.

## Collection and Completion Goals
- Track progress toward quest cape, achievement diary cape, music cape.
- Recommend efficient paths to collection logs.

## Efficiency and Timeboxing
- Provide estimated time to completion for key milestones.
- Suggest session plans with fixed time budgets.
- Offer alternatives when time is limited.

## Risk and Safety
- Flag dangerous activities for current gear/stats.
- Recommend safe training alternatives.
- Suggest death cost mitigation (ring of life, budget gear).

## Ratings and Analytics
- Rate account readiness for specific content.
- Provide bottleneck analysis and top blockers.
- Compare account progress across time snapshots.

## Custom Playstyles
- Support ironman, hardcore, group iron, pure, restricted builds.
- Tune recommendations for preferred playstyle (skilling, bossing, quests).

## Data and Export
- Export checklists, plans, and gear lists to text/JSON.
- Keep local snapshots for progress tracking.

## Coaching UX
- Provide short, readable next steps.
- Explain why each step matters.
- Offer optional deep dives for complex goals.

## Action Execution (Optional)
- Execute mouse and keyboard actions with human-like timing and motion.
- React to on-screen cues with stimulus-aware pacing.
- Support camera control with naturalized movement and corrections.
